How I Work / Process

  1. 1. Consultation – We talk about your goals, target users, design style, features.
  2. 2. Proposal & Quote – I'll send you a detailed scope and cost breakdown.
  3. 3. Deposit – Typically 50% of the quoted amount to start work.
  4. 4. Development – I build the project, keep you updated.
  5. 5. Revisions – Included as per your package. Additional changes billed separately.
  6. 6. Delivery & Launch – Final payment, then deployment or delivery of files.
  7. 7. Support – I'll be available for a short post-launch support period for issues.
